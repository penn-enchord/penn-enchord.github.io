Who Are We?

Penn EnChord is a Western-Chinese fusion singing group at the University of Pennsylvania that started in 2013, dedicated to spreading the love for Chinese music to the Philadelphia community and beyond. The group mixes modern and western singing elements with traditional Chinese music to create a new form of music, a form, which the group hopes will appeal to existing fans of Chinese music, as well as those who have never been exposed before.

安可是谁？

宾大安可合唱团由来自宾大的大陆留学生在 2013 年秋季创建，目前成员包括 23 位费城四校热爱合唱的学生。安可以快乐而又自由，时而严肃时而逗比的演唱态度为宗旨，主打中国风，也融入西方流行。从编曲到合声，我们的歌几乎都是团员一行一行自己写出来的，每一个音符里都有着我们对音乐深深的热爱。对我们来说，合唱团最宝贵的是我们在一次次排练，聚会，演出的欢声笑语中结下的深厚友谊。希望你能从我们的歌声中听到我们对音乐的热爱与对彼此的珍惜。安可，为你安可。

Interested in Joining?

Whether you are from Penn, Drexel, St. Joseph or any other school, and whether you have perfect pitch, or have never had professional music training, as long as you love to sing and think you have a great voice, we'd love to hear from you! Please email us if you would like to schedule an audition.
